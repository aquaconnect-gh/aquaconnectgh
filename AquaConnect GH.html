About AquaConnect GH

Transforming water delivery in Ghana through technology and innovation

Our Story

Founded in 2024, AquaConnect GH began as a student entrepreneurship initiative to solve a critical problem facing Ghanaian communities: unreliable access to clean water.

Our founder, a Ghanaian SHS student, noticed the challenges faced by both water delivery drivers struggling to find customers and households struggling to find reliable water sources. This led to the creation of a simple yet powerful SMS-based platform that connects both sides efficiently.

Today, AquaConnect GH has grown into Ghana's premier water delivery network, serving thousands of homes and businesses across multiple regions while providing sustainable income opportunities for water delivery drivers.
